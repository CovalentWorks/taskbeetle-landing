How it works

Three steps, no chasing.

You stop hunting for the right person. The right people come to you, and compete to do the job well.

1

Post your job

Describe what you need and set your budget. It takes about a minute to get started.

2

Workers bid to win

Skilled and semi-skilled people compete for your job with real bids and ratings. You see who is best, not just who is cheapest.

3

Pay through the Shell

Your money is held safely in escrow and released only when you approve the finished work. No one is left exposed.

The Shell

Money that is protected, not promised.

Every payment locks into the Shell, our escrow, the moment a job is funded. The worker knows it is secured, so they start with confidence. You release it only when the work is right.

  • Funds held the instant a job starts
  • Released only when you approve
  • No one paid for work they did not get
